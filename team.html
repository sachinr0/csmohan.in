Meet The Team

A. Mohan Kumar

Founder

A. Mohan Kumar is a Corporate Lawyer, Practicing Company Secretary and a Cost Accountant. He has more than 20 years of experience as Company Secretary in various Listed and Unlisted Companies including manufacturing, Software and ITES industries.

He has worked in many multi-national Companies and has headed their Legal and compliance divisions successfully for the past two decades.

More details about Mr. Mohan,

  • Mr. Mohan is an Insolvency Resolution Professional and successfully handled many IBC Matters.
  • Mr. Mohan is a Regional council member and Past Chairman of Institute of Company Secretaries of India (Southern India Region). He is the Chairman of few important Committees of – Institute of Company Secretaries of India – Southern Region.
  • Mr. Mohan is a regular speaker and a faculty in forums like Institute of Company Secretaries of India, Institute of Chartered Accountants and Bombay Stock Exchange Ltd.
