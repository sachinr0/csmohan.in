Mohan Kumar & Associates

Mohan Kumar & Associates is a consultancy firm providing Company Formation, other Secretarial and legal services.

We have a team of professionals with a mix of few decades of experience with fresh outlook, who always strive for excellence.


We provide

  • Services for starting a new Company, Partnership firm or MSME
  • Entire gamut of services in the areas of Legal and Secretarial for Limited Companies
  • Services as Legal Counsel for drafting and vetting of contracts
  • RBI related matters (Investment in India) and compliance
  • Interaction on behalf of our client with MCA, ROC, SEBI, NCLT, RBI
  • Conversion of one Form of entity into another Form
  • Merger, Amalgamation and Restructuring
  • Insolvency and Bankruptcy related assistance including acting as Resolution professional
  • Appearance before National Company Law Tribunal for various matters

We have

  • More than 100 clients, serving them on Secretarial, legal, RBI and GST related matters.
  • Network arrangement all over India with other professionals to complete any work across India
  • Clientele which includes Startups, Listed entities, Unlisted Companies, Partnership firms and MSME’s
  • Mix of experienced and young team who proactively advise and help the clients in compliance and in their business operations
