POSH Compliance Services

Creating Safe, Inclusive, and Legally Compliant Workplaces

We specialize in providing end-to-end support for organizations to comply with the Sexual Harassment of Women at Workplace (Prevention, Prohibition and Redressal) Act, 2013 ("POSH Act").

Our mission is to empower companies to foster respectful, inclusive, and equitable work environments through expert guidance, policy development, training, and ongoing compliance support.

SERVICES OFFERED UNDER POSH

  • POSH Policy Drafting and Review
  • Constitution of Internal Complaints Committee (ICC)
  • External Member Services
  • SHE-BOX registration
  • Employee Awareness & Sensitization Sessions (Physical and Online sessions)
  • Training Programs for ICC (Internal Complaints Committee)
  • Annual Report Preparation & Filing
  • POSH Audits & Compliance Checks
  • Presence and participation in conducting inquiry hearings with IC
